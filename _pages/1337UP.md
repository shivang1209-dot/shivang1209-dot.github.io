Intigriti proudly presents the third edition of our 1337UP LIVE CTF. This capture the flag event will have hackers fighting in teams of up to 4 players in a Jeopardy-style event. The event will run for 36 hours, hosting challenges in the usual categories; web, pwn, rev, crypto, game, mobile, misc, osint etc.
